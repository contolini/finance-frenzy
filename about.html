About Finance Frenzy

Finance Frenzy (based on Spaceteam) is a cooperative party game for 2 to 4 players who shout finance-babble at each other until the financial markets implode. Each player needs a computer running the Chrome web browser (we're working hard to bring this to other platforms).

You'll be assigned a random control panel with buttons. You need to follow time-sensitive instructions. However, the instructions are being sent to either you or your teammates, so you have to coordinate before the time runs out. Also, the DOW industrial index is depreciating. And you're trying to outrun the collapsing market.
